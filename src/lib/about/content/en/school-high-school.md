During high school (2018-2020), I attended a chemistry-oriented secondary school in Ostrava.

I intentionally chose this route because I liked chemistry and wanted programming to remain self-directed. Outside school, I kept building software projects, learning from practical problems, and improving faster through iteration than through purely theoretical tasks.

Looking back, this balance was very valuable. School taught discipline and consistency, while self-study gave me speed, curiosity, and real-world problem-solving habits.
