Since 2025, I have been continuing with a master's degree in Computer Science at VSB-TUO, specializing in digital image processing and computer graphics.

This stage expanded my perspective with advanced topics and also introduced me to Rust in a way that strongly influenced my side projects. Alongside coursework, I am building my semestral project and master's thesis around Astro Grader.

Astro Grader processes raw `.fits` images from astro-cameras. In the current phase, it uses conventional quality signals such as FWHM, star count, and background contrast to reject bad frames (for example star trails or cloud contamination). The thesis goal is to replace this statistical scoring with a custom-trained neural model.
